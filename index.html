My name is Fan-Keng Sun (孫凡耕). I am a research assistant in the Speech Processing and Machine Learning Lab at National Taiwan University, under the supervision of Prof. Hung-yi Lee and Prof. Lin-shan Lee. My main interest lies in the intersections of machine learning (ML)/deep learning (DL), electronic design automation (EDA), and computer system.
